    FAQ

    Clear before fans give.

    Is this official?

    No. This is an independent fan-led project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by HYBE, BigHit Music, BTS, RM/Kim Namjoon, Arirang, or any tour organizer.

    How will spending be shown?

    Post receipts, vendor invoices, payment confirmations, budget summaries, and campaign results after each approved spend.

    Can fans help without donating?

    Yes. Fans can volunteer, share approved campaign posts, translate, design assets, distribute flyers, or organize proof-of-spend updates.

    Which payment methods can be connected?

    The page supports USDT, Bitcoin, USDC, and bank transfer paths once approved campaign wallets and bank details are live.
